<meta>Meta tags go here - they are self closing, meaning they don't need a closing tag. Below are the two most common examples of meta tags: 

<meta charset="UTF-8">
<meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1.0">

<title>THIS IS WHERE YOUR TITLE GOES (Needs to be nested inside the head tag)<title>
