About This Page

As an aspiring front-end developer, I am passionate about transforming ideas into visually engaging and highly functional digital experiences. More About This Topic

This website provides a comprehensive and accessible exploration of Web3, examining how the internet is evolving from earlier digital systems into a more decentralised and user-controlled environment.
Throughout the page, I explain what Web3 is in clear, simple language, comparing it to Web1 and Web2 to highlight how digital interactions, data storage, and system control have changed over time.
I discuss how Web3 technologies aim to shift data ownership away from centralised corporations and toward individual users through distributed networks and blockchain systems.

This site also evaluates the role of cryptocurrency in comparison to the Australian Dollar, analysing issues of stability, trust, digital storage, and financial control.
In addition, I explore how blockchain technology functions beyond finance, proposing practical real-world applications, and examine how NFTs represent digital ownership in secure and verifiable ways, including how they could be implemented within a school environment.
Overall, this page is designed to break down complex digital concepts into understandable ideas, helping readers gain insight into the purpose, potential, and challenges of emerging Web3 technologies.
